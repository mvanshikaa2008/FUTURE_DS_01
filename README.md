 📊 RETAIL SALES PERFORMANCE DASHBOARD
 PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project was developed as part of the **Data Science & Analytics Internship (FUTURE_DS_01)**. It presents an interactive Retail Sales Performance Dashboard designed to analyze revenue, customer activity, product performance, and geographical trends. The dashboard enables users to explore key business metrics and gain valuable insights for strategic decision-making.


 🎯 PROJECT OBJECTIVES

* Monitor overall retail sales performance
* Analyze customer and order statistics
* Identify top-selling products
* Compare revenue across different countries
* Track monthly revenue trends and business growth
* Transform raw sales data into meaningful visual insights


🛠️ TOOLS & TECHNOLOGIES

* **Power BI**
* **Microsoft Excel**
* **Python (Pandas)**
* **GitHub**

 📋 PROJECT WORKFLOW

 📈 DASHBOARD HIGHLIGHTS

* Interactive filters for **Country, Invoice Date, and Stock Code**
* KPI Cards displaying:

  * Total Sales
  * Total Customers
  * Total Orders
  * Total Quantity Sold
* Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Top 5 Countries by Revenue
* Business Insights Panel for quick decision support


 💡 KEY INSIGHTS

* **November 2011** recorded the highest revenue among all months.
* **United Kingdom** generated the largest share of total sales.
* Revenue experienced a strong monthly growth of approximately **35%** during the best-performing period.
* A small group of products contributed significantly to overall sales, highlighting high-demand inventory.
* Geographic analysis reveals clear differences in customer purchasing behavior across countries.

 📊 PROJECT OUTCOME

The Retail Sales Performance Dashboard converts complex transactional data into an intuitive and interactive reporting solution. It helps stakeholders monitor business performance, identify sales opportunities, evaluate product demand, and support data-driven decision-making through dynamic visual analytics.
